WHAT IS CORVERSION CALCULATOR ? 

	A conversion calculator is a tool or device, often in the form of a software application or an online tool, designed to facilitate the conversion of units or values from one 
	measurement system to another. These can include conversions between different units of length, weight, volume, temperature, currency, and more. By inputting a value 
	in one unit, users can quickly obtain the equivalent value in another unit, making it convenient for tasks such as cooking, travel, science, or any situation where different 
	measurement systems are used. Conversion calculators are valuable for ensuring accuracy and efficiency in various fields by simplifying the process of converting 
	quantities between different units of measurement.
